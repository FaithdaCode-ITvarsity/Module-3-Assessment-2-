Crispy fried chips, also known as potato chips or crisps, are a beloved snack enjoyed by people all around the world. Their irresistible crunch and savory flavor make them a perfect treat for any occasion. Here’s a closer look at what makes these chips so special and how you can make them at home.

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and slice them thinly. Soak the slices in cold water for 5-10 minutes, then drain and pat dry with kitchen paper.
  2. First Fry: Heat the oil in a deep pan to 130°C. Fry the potato slices in batches for about 10 minutes until they are cooked through but not browned. Remove and drain on kitchen paper.
  3. Second Fry: Increase the oil temperature to 190°C. Fry the potato slices again in batches for 4-5 minutes until they are golden and crispy. Remove and drain on kitchen paper.Seasoning: Sprinkle with sea salt or your preferred seasoning while the chips are still hot.
  4. Serving: Serve immediately for the best crunch.
