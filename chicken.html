Chicken drumsticks

Price: R55

Serving size: 5 ounces


Chicken drumsticks are a versatile and popular cut of poultry, known for their juicy meat and rich flavor. They are the lower part of the chicken leg, consisting of the drumstick and the thigh.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Prepare the chicken: Pat the drumsticks dry with a paper towel.
  3. Season the chicken: In a large bowl, combine olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, sea salt, and black pepper. Toss the drumsticks in the mixture until they are evenly coated.
  4. Arrange on a baking sheet: Place the drumsticks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or foil.
  5. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the skin is crispy.
  6. Serve: Let the drumsticks rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with lemon wedges if desired.

Extra Notes/Requests:

  1. Marination: Marinate the drumsticks for at least 30 minutes to enhance flavor. A simple marinade can include olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and your favorite herbs and spices.
  2. Seasoning: Don’t be afraid to experiment with different seasonings. Smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and dried oregano are great choices.
  3. Crispiness: For extra crispy skin, pat the drumsticks dry with paper towels before seasoning. You can also use a bit of baking powder in your seasoning mix to achieve a crispy texture.
