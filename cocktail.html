A cocktail is a mixed drink, usually alcoholic. Most commonly, a cocktail is a combination of one or more spirits mixed with other ingredients, such as juices, flavored syrups, tonic water, shrubs, and bitters. Cocktails vary widely across regions of the world, and many websites publish both original recipes and their own interpretations of older and more famous cocktails.

Steps

  1. Gather Your Ingredients and Tools:
  2. Alcohol (e.g., vodka, gin, rum, tequila, whiskey)Mixers (e.g., soda, juice, tonic water)Ice Garnishes (e.g., lemon, lime, mint)
  3. Tools: shaker, strainer, jigger (for measuring), muddler (if needed)
  4. Prepare Your Glass:Chill your glass by filling it with ice or placing it in the freezer for a few minutes.
  5. Measure and Pour Ingredients:Use a jigger to measure the alcohol and mixers according to your recipe.Pour the ingredients into a cocktail shaker or mixing glass.
  6. Add Ice:Fill the shaker or mixing glass with ice.
  7. Mix the Ingredients:Shake: If your cocktail includes juice, dairy, or egg whites, shake the mixture vigorously for about 10-15 seconds.
  8. Stir: If your cocktail is all spirits (like a martini), stir gently with a bar spoon for about 30 seconds.
  9. Strain and Serve:Use a strainer to pour the mixed drink into your chilled glass, leaving the ice behind.If your cocktail is served on the rocks, pour it over fresh ice in the glass.
  10. Garnish:Add a garnish like a lemon twist, lime wedge, or mint sprig to enhance the flavor and presentation.
  11. Enjoy:Serve your cocktail and enjoy responsibly!
