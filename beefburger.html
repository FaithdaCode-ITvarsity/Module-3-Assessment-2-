Cheesy Beef Burger

Price: R80

Serving size: 4-6 ounces


A Cheesy Beef Burger is a classic comfort food that combines juicy, seasoned beef patties with melted cheese, all nestled in a soft, toasted bun. This burger is a favorite for its rich flavors and satisfying textures.

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or frying pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties that are slightly larger than the buns. Make sure not to overwork the meat or it will become tough.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper on both sides of each patty.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or frying pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 71°C (160°F) for medium doneness.During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese on top of each patty and let it melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster oven until lightly browned.
  6. Assemble your burger by placing the cooked patty with melted cheese on the bottom bun, followed by lettuce, tomato, onion, and any other toppings you desire. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade cheeseburger!

Extra Notes/Requests:

  1. Extra cheese
  2. No onions
  3. No mayo
  4. Well-done patty
  5. Extra lettuce
  6. Extra ketchup
