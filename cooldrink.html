Refreshing Cooldrink

Price: R25

Serving size: 8-12 ounces


When the sun is blazing and the day feels endless, there’s nothing quite like the crisp, invigorating taste of Pepsi to quench your thirst. With its perfect blend of sweetness and fizz, Pepsi is more than just a drink—it’s a moment of pure refreshment.

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a non-stick saucepan, combine the orange zest, orange peel, lime zest, lemon zest, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, coriander seed, citric acid, and gum arabic.Boil and Whisk: Bring the mixture to a boil, whisking until the gum arabic dissolves.Add Sugar: Add the sugar and bring to a boil for one minute, or until the mixture liquefies.Blend Juices: Remove from heat and blend in the orange, lemon, and lime juices, along with the vanilla extract and browning sauce.Mix with Carbonated Water: Once the mixture cools, mix it with the carbonated water.

Extra Notes/Requests:

  1. Citrus Slices: Lemon, lime, or orange slices can add a zesty twist.Berries: Strawberries, raspberries, or blueberries can add a burst of flavor and color
  2. Herbal Enhancements:Mint Leaves: Fresh mint can make your drink feel even cooler.Basil or Rosemary: These herbs can add a unique and refreshing taste.
  3. Creative Ice Cubes:Fruit-Infused Ice Cubes: Freeze small pieces of fruit in your ice cubes for a colorful and flavorful addition.Herb-Infused Ice Cubes: Freeze herbs like mint or basil in your ice cubes for an extra burst of flavor
  4. Mix It Up:Pepsi Floats: Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream to your Pepsi for a creamy treat.Mocktails: Mix Pepsi with fruit juices like pineapple or cranberry for a refreshing mocktail.
  5. Citrus Zest: Grate some lemon or lime zest over your drink for an aromatic touch.Edible Flowers: Add a few edible flowers for a beautiful and elegant presentation.
