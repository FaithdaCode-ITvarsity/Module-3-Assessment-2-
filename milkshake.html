Creamy Thick Milkshake

A milkshake is a sweet, cold beverage made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings or sweeteners123. The mixture is blended until it becomes thick and creamy3. Common flavorings or sweeteners include butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, or fruit syrup1. Milkshakes are usually served with whipped cream and toppings3. People usually drink milkshakes with a drinking straw and from a tall glass or cup.

Steps

  1. Blend Ingredients: In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract. Blend on medium speed until smooth and creamy.
  2. If you prefer a thicker milkshake, add more ice cream
  3. For a thinner consistency, add more milk.
  4. Pour the milkshake into a chilled glass.
  5. Top with whipped cream, sprinkles, or a cherry if desired.

Extra Notes/Requests:

  1. For a chocolate twist, add a few tablespoons of chocolate syrup before blending
  2. You can also mix in fruits like strawberries or bananas for a fruity flavor.
  3. If you don’t have a blender, you can use an immersion blender or even mix by hand with a whisk (just make sure the ice cream is softened).
